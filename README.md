# Stickforge Arena

A browser prototype for a side-view stickman duel fighter built around forging custom weapons, choosing stage variants, and winning round-based sets against bots or a second local player.

## Run

Open [index.html](./index.html) in a browser.

## Features

- Forge weapons by changing frame, material, edge, length, balance, and temper.
- Save custom weapons to an armory and assign them to Player 1 or the rival slot.
- Pick between duel stages and toggle center hazards, corner pillars, and crowd lights.
- Fight round-based side-view matches with health bars, blocking, jumps, backsteps, and lunges.
- Play either `Player vs Bot` or a local `Player vs Player` duel.

## Controls

- `A` / `D`: walk Player 1
- `W`: jump with Player 1
- `S`: crouch with Player 1
- `Mouse move`: steer Player 1 weapon for light swing damage
- `Left click`: strike with Player 1
- `Right click`: block with Player 1
- `Q`: backstep with Player 1
- `E`: lunge with Player 1
- `J` / `L`: walk Player 2 in duel mode
- `I`: jump with Player 2
- `K`: crouch with Player 2
- `O`: strike with Player 2
- `U`: block with Player 2
- `P`: backstep with Player 2
- `;`: lunge with Player 2
